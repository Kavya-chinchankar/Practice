Maggie recipe

  1. Take water in the pan and bring it to boil. Add Maggi seasoning, salt,turmeric powder and black- pepper powder to it. Add Maggi noodles.
  2. Bring it to boil on high flame and then reduce the heat.
  3. Add tomato ketchup. Allow it to cook on simmer for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add some vegitables:
    • Tomoto
    • Onion
    • Carrot
  5. Now, serve the Maggi noodles.
